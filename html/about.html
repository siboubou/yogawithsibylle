My Yoga Journey
I discovered Yoga travelling around South America, mostly passing by Hare Krishna’s Temples.
While I was initiated to the Vedic Philosophy, the concept of “all is one” and Union of Body, Mind and Soul grew inside me. I started practicing Energetic Therapies through which you can only perceive human and his surroundings in a holistic way.
In 2019, I graduated of 200H Yoga Teacher Training of Vrinda Bhumi School in Verkala - India, where I also learned some of the basis of Ayurveda and Ayurvedic Massages Saatwika Ayurveda Academy.

Yoga gave me peace, but also a structure.
It is a philosophy as much as it is a practice.
A philosophy of acceptance, devotion, union. A practice of discipline and letting it go.
Not only I could feel the physical benefits on my body, stronger, more energized, also I started to feel more grounded and connected. Altogether, I felt better!
This is what I want to share through Yoga and why I have started teaching it.
How I Practice
Some days I like to flow improvising new Vinyasas, some other days I follow a strict Hatha pattern. Sometimes I add up reps or fascia work techniques, and other times I just melt in a Yin practice of restoration. Days I just breath consciously and this is all.
How I Teach
My approach to teach yoga is to make you listen to your body and cultivate body awareness, pointing micro adjustments you can create in a posture, giving you the understanding of where to take the energy from, how to create space in your body.
I give importance to the core and pelvic work as it is fundamental for good posture and relief backpain.
As Every Body Is Different, Each Practice is Unique
Yoga is for everybody, own your practice, it is yours ! Do not bridle yourself, you'll see how good you'll feel after one session.
